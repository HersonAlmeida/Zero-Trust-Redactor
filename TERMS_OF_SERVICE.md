## 2. Description of Service

Zero-Trust Redactor is an open-source, offline document redaction tool that:
- Processes PDF documents locally on your device
- Uses AI models (BERT, Llama) running entirely in your browser
- Identifies and redacts Personally Identifiable Information (PII)
- Operates without sending document data to external servers (one-time model/library downloads only)
